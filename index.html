A developer friendly form builder for React

Wanna see it in action?

Go to Storybook Demo

Fully Extensible

It is not limited to predefined set of ready-made editors , with grommet-hook-form limit is the sky. Rapidly prototype you'r form using the out of the box editors, while having full control over rendering and functionality of each of them and craft your own editor on-demand.

i18 ready

All of editor labels , validation messages , feed-backs , status texts etc are localizable.Its more than easy to integrate it with your localization library.Next js sample is abailable here.

Developer Friendly

Without any extra abstraction layer it is ridiculously easy to adapt with for those who are already familiar with grommet and react-hook-form. Being developed in typescript all of APIs, editors and components are intutive to work with and understand by you and you'r IDE.

Opensource And Free

Its fully open-source and free of charge. It's source is hosted on github and is available as an NPM module.

Performant

Taking advantage of react-hook-form's smart rendering, editors get updated only when necessary which results in smooth and interactive forms.

Extended Validation Support

Almost all type of form validations are possible , lenght , range , pattern and even asynchronous validation.
